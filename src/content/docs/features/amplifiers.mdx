# Three Amplifiers

Aethis includes **three distinct amplifier channels**, each with its own unique tonal character. Switch between amps instantly or dial in your perfect tone with the comprehensive controls available on each.

---

## Noctis — Crystal Clean

<div class="amp-showcase">
  <Image src={noctisAmp} alt="Noctis - Crystal Clean Amplifier" class="amp-image" />
</div>

A pristine, glassy clean amplifier with exceptional headroom and clarity. Noctis delivers bell-like highs and warm lows without any breakup—perfect for jazz, funk, and ambient styles where you need every note to ring true.

**Best for:** Jazz, funk, clean tones, ambient, fingerstyle

---

## Rosaria — Modern High-Gain

<div class="amp-showcase">
  <Image src={rosariaAmp} alt="Rosaria - Modern High-Gain Amplifier" class="amp-image" />
</div>

A dark, powerful amplifier delivering a scooped, aggressive tone perfect for metal and modern rock. Rosaria features boosted bass and treble with scooped mids for that thick, chunky attack that cuts through any mix.

**Best for:** Metal, djent, modern rock, heavy rhythm

---

## Lumina — Classic Rock

<div class="amp-showcase">
  <Image src={luminaAmp} alt="Lumina - Classic Rock Amplifier" class="amp-image" />
</div>

The quintessential rock amplifier tone with a pronounced mid-range punch. Lumina captures that classic British crunch sound—perfect for blues, classic rock, and everything in between.

**Best for:** Blues, classic rock, crunch tones, versatile playing
